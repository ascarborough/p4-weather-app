About This Weather App

Living the vampire life can be difficult - especially when it comes to the weather! The decision on whether to stay in or go out can be a matter of life or death. But if you've found this app, you're in luck! Vampire's Weather App allows you to know the safety on the weather conditions outside immedately. We rely on cloud coverage for our safety and should never be caught outside in direct sunlight. With this app, green means GO and red means STOP - as simple as that.You can also see the safety in the upcoming forecast as well. We hope that this app takes some stress of your life as we take away the guess work.
